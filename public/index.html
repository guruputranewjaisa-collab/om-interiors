Our approach

Rooted in craft, built for today.

Om Interiors was founded in Bengaluru with a simple belief: India's traditional craftsmanship — its carved wood, brass work, and courtyard architecture — has as much to offer a modern home as any imported trend.

Our team works directly with artisan families across Rajasthan, Gujarat and Karnataka, and with a small group of site architects here in Bengaluru, to bring that craft into homes that still work for modern, everyday life.
